Antenna Types and Features

  • Dipole: A widely used, basic antenna with two equal halves.
  • Yagi: A directional antenna offering higher gain, suitable for longer distances.
  • Monopole: A single-element antenna typically mounted on the ground.
  • Loop: A compact antenna with a loop shape, often used for narrow frequency bands.
